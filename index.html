About

Vanaheim Resort in Vagamon, Kerala, is a luxurious haven nestled amidst the serene beauty of nature. This elegant resort offers an escape from the hustle and bustle of daily life, immersing guests in the tranquil surroundings of Vagamon's lush greenery.

The resort boasts a range of luxurious accommodations, each thoughtfully designed to provide comfort and style. From spacious suites to cozy cottages, every detail is meticulously crafted to ensure a relaxing stay. Vanaheim is defined by a harmonious ambiance that resonates with elegance and sophistication.
